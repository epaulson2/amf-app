The Whole-Step Rule

How to find available tensions on ANY diatonic chord — one rule, infinite application.
The Rule
Look at the notes in the key that are NOT part of the chord. Ask: is each note a WHOLE STEP or HALF STEP above the nearest chord tone?

Whole step = Available Tension ✓    Half step = Avoid Note ✗
Worked Example — Imaj7 in C Major (Cmaj7)
