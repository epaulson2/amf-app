Songs That Prove It

"Happy" — Pharrell Williams
2-3 clave. 13/16 stops on STRONG positions. Near-perfect Rhythm Code alignment.
"Mother Popcorn" — James Brown
2-3 clave. The horn stabs and vocal stops map directly onto the 2-3 Rhythm Code map positions.
"Corner of the Earth" — Jamiroquai
2-3 clave in vocal melody. Stop pattern follows the map with remarkable fidelity throughout verse and chorus.
