Worked Example — Bridge in the Key of C
AMF Zone Target
20% Z1 · 25% Z2 · 30% Z3 · 25% Z4

Melody notes: FA–TI–RE–MI over two bars — mostly floating and tense, brief sweet arrival at MI before pulling away again.
HARMONY Chord Pool
Bar 1: Fm7 (iv-7 — parallel minor)
Bar 2: Abmaj7 (bVI — parallel minor)
Bar 3: Bb7 (bVII — backdoor)
Bar 4: G7 (V7 — tension peak before chorus return)
VOICINGS Texture
Guide tones only (3rds + 7ths). Add b9 on G7 for maximum tension. Rhythmic space between chords — don't fill every beat. Let silence carry weight.
Combined Result
FA over Fm7 = CT/root (Zone 2, grounded tension)
TI over Abmaj7 = NCT/#11 (Zone 2, pensive)
RE over Bb7 = NCT/3rd (Zone 3, floating)
MI over G7 = NCT/13th (Zone 2, tense-bright)
