2
Chord relationship Chord tone or non-chord tone?

Zone 1
Sweet / Grounded
Stable + chord tone
Ex: G over C  (SO over C)
Zone 2
Floating / Modern
Stable + non-chord tone
Ex: C over Dm  (DO over Dm)
Zone 3
Tense but Supported
Unstable + chord tone
Ex: F over Dm  (FA over Dm)
Zone 4
Bitter / Edgy
Unstable + non-chord tone
Ex: B over Dm  (TI over Dm)

Writing & Revision Tips
Need arrival?→stable + chord tone
Need modern color?→stable + non-chord tone
Need tension with support?→unstable + chord tone
Need edge?→unstable + non-chord tone
Too safe / generic?→add non-chord tones at key moments
Too harsh?→resolve unstable non-chord tones faster
