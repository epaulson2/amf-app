Stability Axis (Vertical)
How settled is this note in the key? Stable notes (DO, SO, MI) feel like home. Unstable notes (FA, TI, RE) feel like they need to move. This is about the note's place in the key — not the chord.
Chord Relationship (Horizontal)
Is the note part of the chord currently playing? A chord tone has harmonic backing — it "fits." A non-chord tone floats above or collides with the chord — it adds color or tension depending on stability.
Need resolution?
Land on Zone 1 — stable chord tones carry the feeling home.
Need drive / urgency?
Lean into Zone 2 — tense chord tones pull forward without falling apart.
Need dark intensity?
Use Zone 4 — but land back in Zone 1 or it collapses.
Zone placement changes when the chord changes — the same note can move between zones mid-phrase.
