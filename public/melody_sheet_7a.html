Tension Arc Shapes — Three Common Patterns
Classic Rise-Fall
Gentle rise through verse/pre-chorus, climax at bridge, return to resolution. The most universal pop structure.
Genre: Pop, R&B, Singer-songwriter
Slow Build
Tension increases progressively throughout the song — finale is the emotional peak. No release until the very end.
Genre: Epic ballad, film scoring, progressive rock
Explosive Start
Chorus first — maximum impact immediately, then verse provides contrast and depth. Attention-grabbing for streaming.
Genre: Modern pop, dance, viral-optimized tracks
