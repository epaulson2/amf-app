Di-Chord Practice

The Three-Step Cycle — Every S1 Session
HEAR Let the sound land. Don't reach for the label yet. 3–5 seconds of pure listening — no instrument, no naming.
SING Match it with your voice. Both notes, up from the lower. If you can't sing it, you haven't heard it yet.
NAME Now say the bracket name. [3]. Not "minor third" — [3]. The name is the last step, not the first.
"If you can sing it, you own it."
Use this cycle for every di-chord in S1 (10 min).
Never skip SING.  ·  HEAR → SING → NAME → HEAR → repeat.
