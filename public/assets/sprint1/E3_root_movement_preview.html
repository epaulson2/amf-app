The Insight — Plogger to Harmony Connection

When you learn [5] in Sprint 2, you are not learning a new sound. You are naming a sound you already know from the Plogger system. The harmonic movement sequence and the di-chord ear training system are the same ear, operating at two different levels. Every sprint adds one more movement to the map — until every chord change you hear has a number, a color, and a name.
