S4 Free
Play 10 min
FocusImprovise using only owned material
  • Zone 1 melody (A, C, E) over Am — that's your entire vocabulary
  • No new material. No stopping at mistakes. Keep moving.
  • Anything you've drilled this week is fair game
Rule
"If you own it, play it.
If you don't own it,
don't force it."
No tracking
Free Play has no weekly targets. The only measure is: did you play without stopping?
