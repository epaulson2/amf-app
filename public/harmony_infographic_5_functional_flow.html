The 4 Cadences — Where the Arc Breaks or Completes
Authentic
V → I
Arc completes fully. Stage 3 → Stage 4. Strongest resolution possible.
Plagal
IV → I
Arc skips tension. Stage 2 → Stage 4. Gentle, "Amen" feeling — no tritone pressure.
Half
any → V
Arc pauses on tension. Lands on Stage 3. Question mark — listener waits for resolution.
Deceptive
V → vi
Arc twists. Goes to Tonic alternate (vi) instead of I. Plot twist — feels like home but darker.
