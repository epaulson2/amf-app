The 12 Intervals

Every musical distance between two notes — names, sizes, and the emotional quality each carries.

Half Step (Semitone)
The smallest interval in Western music. The distance between any two adjacent notes on a piano (including black keys). E→F and B→C are natural half steps — no black key needed.
Whole Step (Whole Tone)
Two half steps combined. Skip one key on the piano. C→D, D→E, F→G, G→A, A→B are all whole steps. E→F♯ and B→C♯ are also whole steps.
