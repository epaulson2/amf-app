Stay Sweet
Z1→Z1
Note stays a chord tone through the change. No emotional shift — the harmony moves, the note is carried. Creates calm stability at chord changes.
Turn Tense
Z1→Z4
Was supported, now floating outside the new chord. Sudden forward pull — the safe note becomes searching. Best for pre-chorus energy.
Float Free
Z4→Z1
Was floating outside a chord; the new chord claims it. Sudden harmonic support — tension releases as the chord "arrives under" the melody.
Sink Deeper
Z2→Z4
Already tense — new chord makes it worse. Tension escalates at the change. Powerful for bridge climax; needs strong resolution to follow.
The sustained melody note is always RE — only the chord changes, producing four different emotional behaviors
