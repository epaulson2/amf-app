All 4 notes are equal. Any one can be "the root" — divides the octave into 4 equal parts.
Only 3 Unique Dim7 Sounds
C – E♭ – G♭ – A= 4 inversions of the same sound
C♯ – E – G – B♭= 4 inversions of the same sound
D – F – A♭ – B= 4 inversions of the same sound
The other 9 "dim7 chords" you might name are just inversions of these 3 sounds.
