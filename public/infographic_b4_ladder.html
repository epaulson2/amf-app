Start Here — Home Base
DO is the tonal center — the note every other scale degree is measured against. A melody that returns to DO feels resolved. Verse endings on RE or TI; chorus endings on DO or SO.
Maximum Tension — Use With Intent
FA at the top of the ladder isn't a mistake — it's a power tool. Blues, soul, and gospel use FA constantly for its ache. But FA needs resolution. It cannot be a resting point without creating listener fatigue.
Stability order: DO(1) → SO(2) → MI(3) → LA(4) → RE(5) → TI(6) → FA(7) — based on acoustic consonance with the tonic
