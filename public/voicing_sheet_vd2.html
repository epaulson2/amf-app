+1 Voicings

Once voice-leading is in your hands, add ONE tension to every chord. Four questions, then play.
The Four Questions — Answer Them in Order
1
What LEFT HAND STRADDLE?
1 + 7 1 + 9 1 + 5 + 7 1 + 6
2
FULL 7th chord or GUIDE TONES?
Full — R · 3 · 5 · 7 Guide — R · 3 · 7
3
What POSITION based on the previous chord?
P1 P2 P3
Should become automatic with practice — follow the P3→P2→P1 cycle (see Sheet VC1)
4
What available TENSION on this chord?
9 11 #11 13
Reference Sheet VB2 for the available tension on each chord

This is ONE Exercise
Every combination from these four questions produces a beautiful, professional voicing. The options feel loose by design — gravitate toward what you like. Your ear is the judge. Master this exercise and you will sound professional immediately. The tension adds the color; the position handles the voice leading; the straddle establishes the harmonic foundation. All three working together = a complete voicing.
