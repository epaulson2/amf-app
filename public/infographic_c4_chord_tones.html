The Key Insight
The chord under the melody determines which notes feel grounded and which create tension. Change the chord, and the same melody note shifts from resolved to searching — or from tense to anchored.
Zone 1
Sweet
Grounded
Stable + In chord
Maximum harmonic comfort. The note is home and the chord agrees.
Zone 2
Tense
Supported
Unstable + In chord
Chord catches the note but it still wants to move. Forward energy.
Zone 3
Floating
Modern
Stable + Outside chord
Calm in the key but the chord doesn't catch it. Airy and modern.
Zone 4
Bitter
Edgy
Unstable + Outside chord
Wants to move AND the chord doesn't help. Maximum tension.
Zone placement is always relative to both the key AND the current chord — never the note alone
