The Key Insight
RE's stability rank never changes (Rank 5 — Yearning). But RE over G7 is a chord tone (harmonically supported, Zone 1 — the chord catches it), while RE over Cmaj7 is a non-chord tone (floating, Zone 3 — nothing holds it). Same note, same emotional character, fundamentally different experience depending on what the harmony is doing underneath.
