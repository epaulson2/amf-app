Anticipate Beat 3 of the 3-Side
Add an anticipation at "and of beat 2" of the 3-side. This is the final groove signature.

In the 3-side (M2 for 2-3 clave), move the stop that was on beat 3 to the "and of 2" instead. This creates the characteristic funk anticipation that signals to the listener "this is the 3-side."

The 5 Practical Guidelines

1
Even 3 notes can suggest the clave. You don't need a dense melody — sparse patterns can imply the clave just as clearly as busy ones.
2
Not all instruments need to match the clave all the time. Create contrast. Let the verse be loosely clave-aligned; tighten it for the chorus.
3
Build longer grooves using the 2-measure clave as a foundation. 4, 8, and 16-measure cycles all rest on the same 2-measure clave pattern.
4
Verse without clave, chorus with clave creates energy contrast. The tighter clave alignment in the chorus is what makes it "lift off."
5
Your intuition is the final judge. The Rhythm Code is a descriptive system — it describes what works, not prescribes what you must do. If it sounds good, it IS good.
