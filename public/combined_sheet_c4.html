Modal Interchange Zone Shifter

How borrowing a chord recolors the emotional zones of the melody notes landing on it
Harmony OS teaches why you borrow a chord. AMF shows you what that borrowing does to your melody. This sheet connects the two: for each borrowed chord, see exactly how zone placement shifts for the most common melody notes. The note doesn't change. The chord changes. The emotional color changes completely.
