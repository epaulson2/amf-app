Key Insight — Context Determines Everything
The note D is an available tension (9th) on Cmaj7, ii-7, and IVmaj7 — but it is the ROOT of Dm7. The same physical key means completely different things depending on which chord is sounding underneath it. This is why tension theory requires knowing the chord function, not just the note name.
