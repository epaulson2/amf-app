Result
The note arrives already understood — you chose it for reasons, not by accident. Three systems agree on its emotional meaning.
You don't have to run through all 6 levels consciously every time. With practice, levels 3–5 become instinct. The stack is a scaffold to build on — a way of understanding why a note choice works, not a checklist to complete before playing. When you're stuck, start at Level 1 and work down.
