Most Common Zone Shifts — Quick Reference (from Sheet C4)
bVImaj7 (e.g. Abmaj7 in C)
DO → floats (Zone 1 → Zone 3)  ·  MI → pensive (Zone 1 → Zone 2)
SO → stays grounded (Zone 1 → Zone 1)  ·  LA → lands (Zone 3 → Zone 1)
iv-7 (e.g. Fm7 in C)
DO → stays stable  ·  MI → bitter (Zone 1 → Zone 4)
SO → heavy (Zone 1 → Zone 2)  ·  LA → heaviness (Zone 3 → Zone 2)
bVII7 (e.g. Bb7 in C)
DO → floats warm (Zone 1 → Zone 3)  ·  SO → color (Zone 1 → Zone 3)
MI → pensive (Zone 1 → Zone 2)  ·  Very singer-friendly, no avoids.
Lydian II (e.g. D in C)
SO → lifts (Zone 1 → Zone 3)  ·  RE → stays grounded (Zone 1 → Zone 1)
MI → brighter color  ·  Sunshine chord — no darkness at all.
